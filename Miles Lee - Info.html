MILES LEE

Info

  
I’m Miles. I’m working to leverage design, programming, and writing to create rich experiences that foster community.
Born and raised in Dallas, TX, I’m currently at Attentive as a Visual Designer on the Strategic Design Team and a co-Lead for Prism, the LGBTQ+ ERG. In May 2021, I received a BFA from the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts at Washington University in St. Louis (not in Seattle). I majored in Communication Design, minored in Writing and Computer Science, and was a Howard Nemerov Writing Scholar.
In my spare time, I love creative coding, making narrative comics, reverse-engineering dishes from restaurants, birdwatching, and adding to my novelty sock collection.
I’m always open to inquiries, coffee chats, freelance work, commissions, and new opportunities!

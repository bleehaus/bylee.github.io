A High & Lonesome Sound

Getting acquainted with the nine main instruments of Bluegrass Music

20x30 inch Poster, Illustrator, Content Strategy / Spring 2019
This is a poster showcasing the instruments of Bluegrass music. Assigned the topic Bluegrass music, I conducted research to narrow down on one focal point: the instruments.
I learned how to be deliberate with my composition and placement choices. Additionally, since the poster served as an informational piece for those unfamiliar with Bluegrass music, I decided my images needed to carry a lot of information, which my previous imagery wasn't doing.
